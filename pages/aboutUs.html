FAQ

1. What types of photography do you offer?
We specialize in portrait, wedding, event, and creative photography. We also offer professional headshots, branding sessions, and engagement shoots.

2. Where are you based, and do you travel for shoots?
We are based in Kenya, but we are available for local and destination shoots. Travel fees may apply for locations outside our base area.

3. How do I book a session?
You can book a session by contacting us through our website, email, or social media. A deposit may be required to secure your date.

4. What should I wear for my photoshoot?
Wear outfits that make you feel comfortable and confident. Solid colors, neutral tones, and minimal patterns work best. If unsure, we can provide guidance based on your session type.

5. How long does it take to receive my photos?
Turnaround time depends on the type of shoot, but typically:
Portrait & Engagement Sessions: 7-14 days
Weddings & Events: 4-6 weeks

6. Do you edit all the photos?
Yes! We professionally edit and enhance all final images to ensure they meet our high-quality standards.

7. How will I receive my photos?
You will receive your photos via a private online gallery or USB drive (for weddings and larger shoots). Prints and albums are available upon request.
